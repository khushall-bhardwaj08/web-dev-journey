About Mangata & Gallo

Mangata & Gallo specializes in engagement, wedding and anniversary jewelry. The company operates primarily online with a boutique storefront and atelier in Austin, Texas.

Founded by Mariana, an expert in diamond cutting and metal smithing, the brand is known for timeless elegance and high-quality craftsmanship.
